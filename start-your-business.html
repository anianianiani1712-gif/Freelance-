Start Your Business: Make Your Dream Come True

A 55-minute pure-speaking lesson — no grammar, all momentum.

⏱ 55 minutes 👥 5 students 🗣 100% speaking

1. Warm-Up: Business or Bust

6 min

Fast, loud, fun — everyone talks in the first 30 seconds.

Tap New Word. The student has 7 seconds to invent a business using that word out loud — no thinking time, no "correct" answer. Go around twice, faster the second time.

Word Generator
Tap "New Word" to start

2. Idea Spark: Mashup Cards

10 min

Removes the "I don't know what business to choose" freeze.

Each student taps Draw Cards once — an Industry + a Twist = one weird business idea. 2 min to think alone, then 1 min to pitch it informally to a partner. Rotate once.

3. Build Your Dream Business

15 min

Talking framework, not a writing task — 3-4 words max on paper, everything else spoken.

PromptExample
What is it?"A café where…"
Who is it for?"People who…"
What makes it special?"Unlike other cafés, we…"
Slogan3-5 words, punchy

Pair up (2+3). Partner must ask at least 2 questions — "Why would people pay for that?" / "What's your biggest problem?"

4. Investor Roulette

15 min

The energy peak — pitch to the room.

Each student gets 60 seconds to pitch standing up. The other 4 are investors — each must ask one question (rotate: price / competition / target customer / biggest risk). Applaud every pitch, no exceptions.

Pitch Timer
1:00

5. The Investment Round

6 min

Forces real listening and reactive speaking, not memorized lines.

Each student gets 3 coins. They invest in other people's businesses — split or go all-in. Add each business below and tap + to invest a coin. Then ask 2-3 students: "Why did you invest there?"

Live Investment Tracker

6. Closing Round: One Step Forward

4 min

Ends on a forward-looking, energetic note.

Lightning round — one sentence each: "You just got funded. What's the very first thing you do tomorrow?"
