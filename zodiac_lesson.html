✨ Zodiac Signs & Guessing Game ✨

Learn the signs, watch real people guess them, then test your own intuition

1

Warm-Up: Believer or Skeptic?

5–7 min
Goal: find out who in the group believes in horoscopes and who doesn't — get everyone talking before the topic starts.
Everyone clicks their button once. Look at the score together, then discuss in pairs:

🔮 If you believe...

  • Do you know your Sun, Moon, and Rising sign?
  • Has a horoscope ever been surprisingly accurate?
  • Do you check your horoscope before big decisions?

🙄 If you're skeptical...

  • Why do you think astrology is so popular?
  • Have you ever secretly checked your horoscope anyway?
  • What convinces people something is "so true"?

Now YOU Guess the Zodiac Sign!

15–18 min
Goal: as a group, vote on each person's zodiac sign, then reveal the real answer.
